About Me

I’ve always had a passion for building and creating. Be it model kits as a child, woodworking projects in middle school, the web design classes I took in high school, or the construction work I did as a young adult. I found no greater joy than in the reward I discovered in solving complex issues, learning the necessary skills for a specific project, and looking back at a completed project with a thought of “Is this complete though? How can I improve this?”. This reflection is what led me to attend St. John’s University to achieve a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I received Dean’s List accolades for the entirety of my time in the program and achieved a 3.7 GPA. I received valuable knowledge and hands on experience in a plethora of technologies including, but not limited to:

  • Java
  • C
  • HTML/CSS/Javascript
  • Bootstrap
  • Node.js
  • SQL/MySQL

I’m looking for opportunities to apply, and grow, my knowledge and experience in designing and developing software. Please connect with me on Linkedin, Github, or through any other means and feel free to contact me
